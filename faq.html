Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for items in their original condition. Please contact us to initiate a return.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the United States. International shipping may take longer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping for an additional fee. Please indicate that you would like gift wrapping at checkout.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. Please contact us to cancel your order.

How can I track my order?

We will send you a tracking number once your order has shipped. You can use this tracking number to track your package on the carrier's website.
